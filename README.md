# Nema17-Sheetmetal-Motor-Mounts-CoreXY-
A rigid sheet-metal motor mount for NEMA17 motors in CoreXY printers. Its large aluminum surface improves convective cooling and acts as a thermal buffer, spreading heat evenly. The design offers high stiffness, low deformation, and easy, cost-efficient manufacturing. UserMod for/based on DuEnder by Sergei Irbis.
